Facts about gender wage gaps

  • According to surveys done by Randstand US, 80% of women would switch to companies that have more gender equality
  • In an Ontario government report, women with the same credentials as men earn on average $7200 less than their male counterparts
  • If current trends continue, it will take 217 years for the gender gap to close
  • If equal pay is acheived between male and female workers, it will cut women poverty rate by 50%
  • Due to women's lower earning power, they more likely to stay in abusive relationships because they feel like they won't be able to support themselves

Facts about racial wage gaps

  • Canadian Centre for Policy Alternatives compares census data of 2006 to 2016. There hasn't been much change.
  • Racialized women earn 59 cents for every dollar by white men, ratialized men earn 78 cents for every dollar by non racialized peoples
  • Visible minorities have much higher chances of experiencing discimination in the workplace
  • Resumes from english names are 35% more likely to receive callbacks

What to do after discovering a wage gap?

  • Contact others who are apart of your workplace to try the website and see the results to make sure that it is not just an individual issue
  • Create a petition if it looks like there seem to be multiple people who have gotten results that show that their salary is lower than average
  • Address these issues to your manager as a group
